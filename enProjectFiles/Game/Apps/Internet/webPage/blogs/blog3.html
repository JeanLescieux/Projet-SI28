Some Books for You

Some Book Recommendations ^^

"Mind Children: The Future of Robot and Human Intelligence" (1988) by Hans Moravec, which discusses the possibility of transferring the human mind into machines, anticipating many transhumanist ideas on enhancement and immortality.

"Engines of Creation: The Coming Era of Nanotechnology" (1986) by K. Eric Drexler. This visionary book introduces the concept of nanotechnology and its implications for the future of humanity, including transhumanist themes like life extension and human enhancement.

The Prospect of Immortality, Robert Ettinger
