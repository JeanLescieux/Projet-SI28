Alternative Ways to Support Life?

I have a lot of ideas but I'm not sure how feasible they are.

1 - Photosynthesis. Humans would genetically modify themselves to use photosynthesis to sustain their life. From what I currently understand, photosynthesis is not efficient enough to produce the energy needed for an active organism.

But what if we genetically modify or create a life form or plant that is incredibly efficient, capable of achieving between 50 and 100% efficiency using photosynthesis? I have very little knowledge on this subject, but with such efficiency and energy generation capacity, could a human theoretically apply these genes to themselves, becoming a plant-person who wouldn’t need much, or any, external source of sustenance?

2 - Solar Cells. Instead of using plant DNA or organic material for photosynthesis, a person could have nanobots of self-replicating solar panels in their body. These solar panels would generate electricity, which the body could then convert into fuel to sustain life.
